Privacy Policy for BTScan

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

BTScan is a Bluetooth scanning utility designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what information the app accesses and how it is used.

Information We Collect

BTScan does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data handled by the app remains entirely on your device.

Bluetooth Data

BTScan uses your device's Bluetooth hardware to detect nearby Bluetooth devices that are actively broadcasting. For each detected device, the app may display:

  • Device name (if broadcast by the device)
  • A Bluetooth identifier (UUID)
  • Signal strength (RSSI)
  • Manufacturer information (if broadcast by the device)
  • Device type, inferred from publicly advertised Bluetooth service identifiers

This information is read directly from public Bluetooth advertisement signals in your vicinity. BTScan does not connect to, pair with, or extract any data from the devices it detects.

Notes

BTScan allows you to attach personal notes to specific devices for your own reference. These notes are stored only in your device's local storage and are never transmitted anywhere.

Data Storage

All data — including scan results and any notes you create — is stored locally on your device using standard system storage (UserDefaults). No data is:

  • Sent to external servers
  • Shared with third parties
  • Used for advertising or analytics
  • Backed up to any service controlled by the developer

If you delete the app, all associated data is removed from your device.

Third-Party Services

BTScan does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Bluetooth Permission

BTScan requests Bluetooth access solely to perform its core function: scanning for and displaying nearby Bluetooth devices. This permission is required by iOS and macOS and is used for no other purpose.

Children's Privacy

BTScan does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, as it does not collect information at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be made available with the app and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.
